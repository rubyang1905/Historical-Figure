Timeline of José Rizal

June 19, 1861

José Rizal was born in Calamba, Laguna, Philippines.

1877

Rizal graduated from the Ateneo Municipal de Manila with a degree in Bachelor of Arts.

1884

He earned his degree in Medicine from the Universidad Central de Madrid in Spain.

1891

Rizal published his second novel, "El Filibusterismo".

1892

He founded the La Liga Filipina, a civic organization aimed at promoting reforms in the Philippines.

1896

Rizal was arrested and later executed on December 30, 1896, in Bagumbayan (now Luneta Park).
